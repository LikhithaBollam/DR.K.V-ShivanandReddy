Dr. K. V. Shivanand Reddy: Leading Neurosurgeon, Researcher, and Award Recipient

I am one of the best Consultant Neurosurgeons with immense knowledge and experience in treating all types of Neuro diseases with utmost proficiency. I have a keen interest and expertise in the management of Complex brain surgeries, Neurovascular surgeries, Minimally invasive spine surgeries, Emergency Brain and spine trauma surgeries, and Stroke surgeries. I have extensive experience in performing complex brain and spine surgeries such as Neuro-vascular surgeries, Minimally invasive spine surgeries, Brain and spine trauma surgeries, Stroke treatment, Brain aneurysm surgeries, Complex spine surgeries, Skull base tumors, Neuro-oncology, Epilepsy surgeries, and Deep brain stimulation, among others. Apart from my clinical expertise, I have been actively involved in research work and academics and have numerous papers, presentations, and publications to my name. I am a recipient of various national and international awards including the Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Award 2021 for Young Achiever in Neurosurgery. I am an active member of the Neurological Society of India (NSI), Telangana Neuroscientists Association (TNSA), and Andhra Pradesh Neuroscientists Association (APNSA).
