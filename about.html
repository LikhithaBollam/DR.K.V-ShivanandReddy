Special Interest And Expertise:

Brain aneurysm surgeries

Brain aneurysm surgeries are medical procedures performed to treat brain aneurysms, which are weak spots in the walls of blood vessels in the brain that can balloon and potentially rupture, causing bleeding into the brain (hemorrhagic stroke) or surrounding area.

Skull base Tumors

Skull base tumors are growths that develop at the base of the skull, which is the area that supports the brain and connects it to the spinal cord and facial structures. These tumors can arise from various types of tissue, including bone, nerves, blood vessels, or the covering of the brain (meninges).

Neuro-oncology

Neuro-oncology is a specialized field of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of brain tumors and other cancers of the nervous system. These tumors can arise from various types of cells within the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Epilepsy surgeries

Epilepsy surgery is a specialized treatment option for individuals with epilepsy whose seizures are not well controlled with medications or other conservative therapies. The goal of epilepsy surgery is to identify and remove or disconnect the area of the brain responsible for generating seizures, thereby reducing or eliminating seizure activity and improving the patient's quality of life.
